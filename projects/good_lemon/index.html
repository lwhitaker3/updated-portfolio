About

Travel + Leisure said Miami is the rudest city in America, so we wanted to prove them wrong. We know that Miami is generous, and when it is, it’s contagious.

At our non-profit stand in Coconut Grove, Florida, you can treat yourself to one of our artisanal lemonades for the price of $5. But, we also encourage you to pay it forward and make someone’s day extra sweet.

If you pay it forward, you contribute to our chain of goodness (yay!), and you’re dubbed a #GoodLemon. You can see how the chain is doing any time at our stand and on our How Good is the Grove graph.

With all proceeds going to the Pulse Orlando fund, we hope to spread as much generosity as we can.
