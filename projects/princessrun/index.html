Princess Run

Princess Winnifred must collect the water orbs to save the kingdom, but Lilibelle, the dragon queen is trying to stop her. Run through Kalaria and collect the water (blue) orbs, but don't let the fire (red) orbs hit you.

Use the left and right arrow keys to run forward and backward. Jump with the up arrow. Don't fall into the water or you'll die. If you die just click in the game screen and the game will restart.
